こんにちは! (Hey!) I’m Naruto Uzumaki, a Hidden Leaf Ninja and your next Hokage.
I like instant ramen in a cup, and I really like the ramen Iruka Sensei got me at the Ichiraku Noodle Shop, but I hate the three minutes you have to wait after you pour the water in the ramen cup. My hobby is eating different kinds of ramen and comparing them, and my future dream is to be the greatest Hokage! Then the whole village will stop disrespecting me and start treating me like I'm somebody. Somebody important!

When people are protecting something truly special to them they can become as strong as they can be. I am strong because I have people to protect. Because they saved me from myself, they rescued me from my loneliness. They were the first to accept me for who I am. They're my friends.
My power isn’t the Rasengan or Sage Jutsu or the Nine Tails chakra. My power is right here: my heart. Until I surpass my dreams, I refuse to die. I'm not gonna run away, I never go back on my word! That's my にんど: my ninja way!

Hidden Leaf Academy:

Graduated as Genin
Honed skills in taijutsu, ninjutsu, and genjutsu: In case you didn't notice earlier, my Sexy Technique Jutsu is unmatched in the entire village. I think it’s important to be adaptable, and persuasive - this jutsu is key to taking you there! I also perfected my Shadow Clone Jutsu while attending classes, giving you not one - not ten - but hundreds of copies of myself. More to love! I also learned how to run up trees while attending the academy, which proved to be the most challenging of all.
Served on missions for the Hidden Leaf Village: under the careful watch of Kakashi Sensei, I joined Team 7 in spending day after day helping the Village with mundane tasks such as weeding gardens, locating lost kitties, and defeating Zabuza, the Demon of the Hidden Mist.

Post-Academy Training:

Apprenticed under Jiraiya Sensei
Gained ability to control Nine-Tails chakra: Some people say that trial and error is the key to success. I have to agree! Jiraiya's patience in letting me get angsty and lash out (literally) was fundamental practice to learn how to tame the beast inside (also literally). Without this training, I would have never been able to get Kurama to acknowledge me.
Conducted research: I'd rather not go into detail about this, but you can read more about it in the gripping novel Icha Icha - Makeout Paradise. Or just ask Kakashi Sensei for a synopsis.
Trained in Sage Art: What they tell you is that you'll unlock powers beyond your wildest dreams; what they don't tell you is that it takes a whole lot of sitting around to get there first.
Realized what it means to be a gutsy ninja: Without Pervy Sage, I'd be lost.

Fourth Great Ninja War:

Served on the Frontlines
Sent in the Clones: Nothing can pull me away from learnin', but on the battlefield my Jinchuriki aura is burnin'! Um... I mean, while I was training with Killer Bee, I split myself into Shadow Clones to go assist my friends and allies. Like any good ninja would do!
Saved the world from Infinite Tsukuyomi: Amidst the twists and turns of the War, the Infinite Tsukuyomi was only a piece of the puzzle. Still, living in a fluffy fake-reality? Dream on! Kaguya couldn't stand a chance against Team 7 - all it takes is strategy, strength, and the bonds of friendship.
